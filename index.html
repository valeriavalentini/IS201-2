About

Discover Puglia's charming towns, where historic architecture meets crystal-clear Adriatic waters. Wander through whitewashed streets, savor exquisite cuisine, and bask in the Mediterranean sun.

Choose and Relax!

Travelling with someone who knows where to go changes the experience of travelling. When booking with Puglia Unfiltered, you will experience the great joys of "living" in the region as someone who knows it like its own city!

Towns

From the iconic trulli of Alberobello to the stunning baroque of Lecce, Puglia offers an enchanting escape into Italy's rich culture and natural beauty.

Beaches

Puglia's beaches are a paradise for sun-seekers. Dive into the turquoise waters of the Ionian and Adriatic Seas, explore hidden coves, and relax in the serene beauty of Italy's most stunning coastal landscapes.
