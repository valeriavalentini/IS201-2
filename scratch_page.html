Ingredients (10 people)

  1. Dough Ingredients
    • Flour 00 - 250g
    • Warm Water - 285g
    • Yeast - 8.5 g
    • Salt - 10g
    • Manitoba Flour - 250g
    • Extra Vergin Olive Oil - 8g
    • Sugar - 5g
  2. Filling Ingridients
    • Mozzarella - 250g
    • Salt - as needed
    • Olive Oil - as needed
    • Tomatoe Paste - 200g
    • Oregano - as needed
  3. To Fry
    • Vegetable Oil - as needed

Recipe

To get the Panzerotti ready, start working on the dough. Pour the flour 00 in a bowl with the manitoba flour. Add the yeast and the sugar.
Add the warm water slowly to activate the yeast. Start mixing while adding the salt.
Add more water before mixing again and then add the oil.
Knead the dough with your hands. When it is all mixed transfer it on the table and keep kneading it with you hands until it is homogeneous.
After, divide it in ten pieces of 80 g each. Form little balls and then put them in a clean surface to cover them and let them rise. Cover them with a canvas and leave them for 2 hours, unitl they have doubled in size.
While the dough is rising get the filling ready. Cut the mozzarella and put it in a strainer so that the water is relased. Add the tomate paste, the salt, and the oregano and put it in the fridge.
When the dough is risen, grab one ball and use a rolling pin to get a circular shape. Add the filling in the middle and close the panzerotto with half-moon shape. Press on the border to get the air out and make sure it is closed.
In a pan, add the vegetable oil. When hot and ready, put the panzerotti until the dough is golden.

Enjoy!!!
